I'm Abigail Seabrook, an artist and storyteller passionate about creativity and teaching. Over the years, I've explored many forms of art, from modeling to photography and set design, and I love bringing ideas to life.

I've found fulfillment in working with kids, inspiring them to tap into their creativity and look beyond the screen. Outside of teaching, I focus on photography, creative directing, and set design, always stepping outside my comfort zone to discover new passions.

My journey has taught me that purpose isn't about doing something big, it's about making meaningful connections and growing along the way.
